New Exhibit?

On display from October of 2024 through March of 2025, Maurice is an elder Glorpus from southern Cambodia. He is the result of a failed genetic experiment on Kangaroos, making him exceptionally rare. Maurice presents a once in a lifetime opportunity to learn about the Glorpus species. This unusual piece of history is on display for a limited period of time so come in and check him out before he is gone!

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire curiosity by presenting unseen organisms from around the planet. From failed genetic experiments to creatures recovered from crashed spacecraft, our unique collection of organisms is guaranteed to inspire curiosity and leave you with more questions than answers.
