No Wheels, No Problem

The brand new 2025 Phord Floatus model uses magnetic technology to slide frictionlessly above the ground. The advantage to this technology over standard wheels is priceless. No tire change or rotation needed, no worry of sliding in snowy conditions, 0 degree turning radius, the list goes on.

Automatic Emergency Braking

All 2025 Floatus models include automatic emergency braking in the base package. Our new technology allows the Floatus to brake quicker than any car in its class using the magnetic systems where the wheel would typically be. The 2025 Phord Floatus was voted as the safest sedan in its class for 2025 by Kelly Blue Book thanks to not only this technology but also the hundreds of other safety measures that we took to make this the safest car on the road.

Advanced Crash Detection System

All 2025 Phord models come equipped with our advanced crash detection system, even at the base level. Accidents are not always avoidable but when they do happen it is crucial to get an emergency response team to the crash as quickly as possible. Our crash detection system automatically dials 911 when it detects a crash as well as sending the location of the crash to the 911 operator, ensuring that help will be on the way even if the driver is unconscious.
