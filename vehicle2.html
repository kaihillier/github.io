Why 4 Wheels when you can have 6?

The Phord F-6 offers a new driving experience. By adding a 3rd wheel in the middle of the truck, the towing capacity is increased by 14% while torque and acceleration are also increased. The Phord F-6 is a smooth driving experience on and off the road, resulting in the most versatile truck on the market.

Versatility You Can Feel

Built for towing and designed for comfort, the all new 2025 Phord F-6 offers the most versatile truck experience on the market. With over 14,000 lbs. of towing capacity and nearly 1,100 ft. lbs. of torque the F-6 is a towing beast for any job. Interior packages include options for full leather or suede seats which creates a vehicle that is luxurious and powerful. Better than any truck on the market and more versatile than any SUV, the Ford F-6 is for everyone.

Crash Detection System

All 2025 Phord models come equipped with our advanced crash detection system, even at the base level. Accidents are not always avoidable but when they do happen it is crucial to get an emergency response team to the crash as quickly as possible. Our crash detection system automatically dials 911 when it detects a crash as well as sending the location of the crash to the 911 operator, ensuring that help will be on the way even if the driver is unconscious.
