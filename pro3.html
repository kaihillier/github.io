A History of Excellence

Since 1928, Phord Motors has been designing and manufacturing award winning cars for the American people. From Freddy Phord’s first concept car in 1927 to our new 2025 models, Phord has grown into America’s trusted car brand. While we have come a long way and gone through considerable change throughout the years, one thing remains true: Phord is the brand for everyone. From the everyday hero working towards the American dream to CEOs and millionaires, Phord is the brand for you.
